How to met my love

by Minh

Your Dating Agency brought me a lot of happy memories. My husband, David, and I just celebrated our 6th wedding anniversary a couple of weeks ago, having met through the agency. I took the opportunity to look at the website again after having talked to you, and it was lovely to see how much better developed it now is, and I particularly like the bit on "love stories". In the first few months I was quite disappointed, as I did not have any dates - and I was starting to feel quite down. But just as I considered leaving and to forget it all, my advisor asked me to hold on a little longer - and I am not quite sure what she did, but it worked.
